Welcome

Enjoy all the activities
that this place has to offer.

In the downtown area of the City of San José del Cabo, you will find a large part of the history and culture of the region, which make this place a place to know and enjoy. Surrounded by the iconic style that the city represents, visitors identify and connect with walks full of stories, legends, art, culture, flavors and unique views of the place.

You can walk calmly and enter the bars, restaurants, galleries, boutiques among others; and enjoy the relaxed atmosphere of the city, maybe have a complementary tequila, wine or cocktail, maybe some sandwiches, cheese or nuts, and at the same time learn a little more about the history of the region.
